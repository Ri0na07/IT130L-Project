✨ About ExploreEasy

Our Story

ExploreEasy Travel and Tours & Art Collections was founded in February 2025 in Looc, Dumaguete City, Negros Oriental. We started with a simple idea: to make travel planning easy, stress-free, and accessible for everyone.

What began as a small venture has grown into a trusted travel service provider, helping hundreds of travelers discover the best destinations across the Philippines and beyond. We believe travel is more than just visiting places — it’s about creating stories, connecting with nature, and embracing new cultures.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯 Mission: To unfold the dreams of people ready to embark on the adventure of a lifetime into reality, through reliable, affordable, and personalized travel solutions.

👁️ Vision: To become the most preferred travel partner in the region, known for excellent service, creativity, and commitment to customer satisfaction.
